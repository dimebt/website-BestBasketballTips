OUR APP

Best Basketball Tips mobile app gives users selected predictions for basketball matches including NBA, NCAA, Euroleague, EuroBasket, Europe Champions League, ABA League, Super Ligi, ACB, Lega A, LNB, BBL and many others basketball leagues. Tips are carefully analyzed and selected by experienced team of 3 professional basketball tipster: Darko, Dimche and Jovce. The tipsters are real persons and they will post daily free and paid VIP Tips and this app will look like a competition between each other. We will rank our tipsters monthly by their success rate of Vip Tips in the Success Rate section in the app. Every match has information about home and away team, tournament type, local start date and time, match status, match scores, tipster, type of the tip, odds, publish date and time. Current recap/status of the prediction (WIN/LOSE) can be viewed by tapping on the current match.
